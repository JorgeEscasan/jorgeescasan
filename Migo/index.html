Say Hello to Migo!

The way people used computers yesterday, today.

(But easier)

What is Migo?

Migo is an interactive, easy-to-use command line that anyone can use. No programming knowledge necessary.

Why do I need Migo? Can't I just click on stuff?

Maybe, but using Migo allows you to develop a better understanding of how computers work and allows you to increase your functionality when using a computer.

So how does Migo work?

Simply tell Migo what you want to do and Migo will do it for you!

What OS does Migo work with?

As of right now Migo is in its testing phase for OSX, once Migo for Mac is stable, I will begin to work on versions for Linux and Windows.
